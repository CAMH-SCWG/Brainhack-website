What?

Brainhack is a global conference bringing together researchers from multiple disciplines to work together on innovative projects related to neuroscience. Brainhack Toronto will feature presentations from key researchers in our community as well as video links to the global community. Visit BrainHack Global 2017 to learn more about events worldwide.
